Why do cats love catnip?
Catnip is an herb from the mint family. It grows naturally in Europe, Asia, and the Middle East, and it’s long been renowned for its effect on cats. The oil in the leaves of the plant contains a compound called “nepetalactone,” which enters your cat’s brain through the nose or mouth. It then creates a chemical reaction in the brain and triggers your cat’s pheromone receptors.
